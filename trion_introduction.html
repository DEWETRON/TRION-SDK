Introduction to TRION Measurement Systems¶

https://www.dewetron.com/products/signal-conditioning-amplifiers

High precision signal conditioning amplifiers¶

DEWETRON offers a wide range of analog, isolated signal conditioning amplifiers, suitable for a wide variety of sensors, including strain gauges, accelerometers, force sensors, pressure, load and flow sensors, thermocouples, as well as voltages and currents.

Multifunctional High-speed Modules¶

Enjoy the full flexibility of DEWETRON multifunctional high-speed modules to maximize channel density, practicability and speed. Choose from different models that are compatible with our brand-new DEWE3 or DEWE2 series.

Isolated highspeed data acquisition for every sensor & every application¶

TRION and TRION3-18XX-MULTI¶

It can primarily be used, where precision, speed and dynamic is needed, like mechanical structure analysis, vibration and shock testing as well as any other measurement task.

Compatible with our brand new DEWE3-series to achieve the highest performance of 5 MS/s or with DEWE2 series for highspeed applications with a limited number of inputs.

10+ measurement modes for almost every sensor and application:

  • 9 native modes: bridge, voltage, current, IEPE, resistance, potentiometer, temperature (RTD), counter and CAN

  • MSI compatibility for charge, thermocouple and LVDT input

The most user friendly data acquisition software OXYGEN will lead you to very quick and reliable measurement results.

Power High-speed Modules¶

High-speed power modules of the TRION™/TRION3™ series for 4-phase power analysis. These user-exchangeable signal conditioning modules provide analog-digital conversion on each channel.

TRION and TRION3-18XX-POWER¶

Key facts

  • Simultaneous sampling for all analog inputs

  • Separate ADC on each channel up to 10 MS/s

  • High resolution up to 18-bit, with anti-aliasing filters

  • High voltage and current inputs

High-speed TRION™/TRION3™ Power Modules¶

High-speed power modules of the TRION™/TRION3™ series for 4-phase power analysis. These user-exchangeable signal conditioning modules provide analog-digital conversion on each channel.

TRION™ Amplifier Modules¶

The user exchangeable, multichannel TRION™ modules provide analog-digital conversion on each channel.

Multi channel TRION modules¶

Multi channel TRION™ modules with A/D converter¶

Multi channel TRION™ signal conditioning modules provide analog-digital conversion on each channel and are user exhangeable.

TRION™ modules are usable in DEWE2 and DEWE3 systems.

Key facts

  • Simultaneous sampling for all analog input modules

  • Separate ADC on each channel

  • 24 bit resolution, with anti-aliasing filters

  • Synchronous counter/encoder inputs and digital I/O lines

  • CAN, counter, digital I/O and timing modules
